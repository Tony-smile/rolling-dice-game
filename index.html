Rules Of The Game

  • First Player to Accumulate a Total Point of 100 Wins the Game
  • Current Player Possess rolling Power as many times as possible provided he/she doesn't roll 1 dice
  • When a Player rolls 1 dice they loses rolling Power to the opponent as well as unheld points
  • When a player Holds accumulated Current point it's added to their total point and they lose their rolling Power to the opponent
  • Good luck as you Play
